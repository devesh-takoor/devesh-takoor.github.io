About Me

I'm Devesh Takoor, and I'm currently working at Software Concepts Ltd as an Associate Software Engineer. As a Full Stack Developer, I am enthusiastic about all aspects of programming and willing to master new and existing programming skills. As a participant and now as a mentor, I have been representing Mauritius in the FIRST Global Challenge (Olympics of Robotics) since 2019. I am also a performing artist (musician and actor in short films, sketches, and skits) at various levels(nationally and internationally). In addition, I am a nature lover who spends the most of my time outdoors (forest, lakes, mountains, deep-sea explorations). Possess strong critical thinking and problem-solving abilities that aid in the resolution of nearly every difficulty encountered during projects. Have the capability to manage a team and distribute it properly.
